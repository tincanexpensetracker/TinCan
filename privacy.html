This Privacy Policy explains how the TINCAN mobile application (“TINCAN”, “the app”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using TINCAN you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1

Who we are

TINCAN is a personal savings and budgeting app that also supports shared (group) savings between members. The app is developed and maintained by an independent developer. For any privacy questions, contact us at customercare@tincan.anonaddy.com.

2

Using TINCAN without an account (guest mode)

You can use TINCAN without signing in. In guest mode, the savings, transactions, budgets, and recurring payments you create are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information is permanently deleted.

3

Information we collect

We collect the following only when you choose to use the relevant features:

  • Account information. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, Google account identifier, and (if available) your profile photo, via Google’s sign-in service.
  • Financial information you enter. The savings goals, transactions, amounts, budgets, recurring payments, and notes you create in the app.
  • Profile picture. If you set one, it is uploaded and stored so it can appear on your profile and to other members of groups you join.
  • Shared / group data. If you create or join a group, your display name, profile photo, and the shared transactions, balances, and transfers within that group are visible to other members of the same group.
  • Notifications. With your permission, we send reminders and updates (for example, recurring-payment reminders). You can disable these at any time in your device settings.
  • Payment reminders. When you create a payment reminder, the title, notes, time, and repeat settings you enter are saved only on your device and are used to schedule a local notification. This reminder content is never uploaded to us or any third party, and it stays on the device even if you are signed in.

We do not collect your contacts, location, photos library, SMS, or call logs. We use the information described above only to run the app for you. Your display currency is taken from your device’s region/language settings (or the currency you pick manually in Settings) — we do not use GPS or location to determine it, and the choice stays on your device / in your account settings.

Device permissions for reminders. Payment reminders use your device’s notification and vibration features, and the chosen alert tone, to nudge you at the time you set. The alert (sound, vibration, or silent) follows your phone’s ringer profile unless you choose otherwise in the app’s reminder settings. Reminders use battery-friendly scheduling, so they may arrive a few minutes around the time you chose, and they respect your phone’s Do Not Disturb and silent modes.

4

How we use your information

  • To provide core app functionality — tracking your savings, budgets, and transactions.
  • To sync your data across your devices when you are signed in.
  • To enable shared savings features within groups you choose to join.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To send notifications you have opted into.

We do not sell your personal information.

5

Where your data is stored

When you are signed in, your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage), which act as our data processor. Data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit. Firebase is operated by Google; their handling of data is governed by the Firebase Privacy and Security policy and the Google Privacy Policy.

6

Data sharing

We share data only as follows:

  • With Google Firebase, as our infrastructure provider, solely to operate the app.
  • With other group members, but only the data within groups you choose to create or join.
  • Where required by law, to comply with a valid legal obligation.

We never sell or rent your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

7

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your data at any time. Deleting an item, transaction, or group removes it. Guest-mode data is removed by clearing app data or uninstalling — it never leaves your device, so there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

7a

Request account & data deletion

If you signed in to TINCAN, you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, whether or not you still have the app installed.

Option 1 — In the app (instant): open Settings → Account → Delete account. This immediately and permanently deletes:

  • Your profile, profile picture, and sign-in record
  • All transactions, budgets, recurring payments, piggy banks, and custom categories
  • Your membership in any shared/family groups (other members’ data is unaffected)

Option 2 — Without the app: email customercare@tincan.anonaddy.com from the address associated with your account and ask us to delete it. We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days, deleting the same data listed above.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We do not retain backups of deleted account data beyond what is needed to fulfil legal obligations (for example fraud prevention), and any such residual data is deleted on the same schedule as our standard backup rotation.

8

Security

We rely on Google Firebase’s security infrastructure, including encryption of data in transit and access controls restricting your data to your authenticated account and the groups you belong to. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9

Children’s privacy

TINCAN is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Most of these are available directly in the app; for anything else, contact us at the email below.

11
