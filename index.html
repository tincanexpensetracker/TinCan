What you can do with TINCAN

A complete money toolkit — from your first rupee saved to shared family goals.

🧮

Smart monthly budgets

Plan every month with the proven 50/30/20 “Golden Rule” — Needs, Wants and Savings — then watch your spending track live against each category. Apply a plan to future months in one tap.

🐷

Savings goals & piggy banks

Create goals for the things you’re saving for, set a target, and watch each piggy bank fill up as you contribute. Celebrate the moment you hit 100%.

👨‍👩‍👧

Group & family saving

Start a group, invite family or friends by email, and save toward shared goals together. Shared balances, transactions and transfers stay in sync for every member in real time.

🔥

Daily streaks & medals

Log an entry each day to grow your savings streak. Earn medals from Sprout to Legend, unlock milestone celebrations, and let our friendly tin-can mascot cheer you on.

🤝

Friends

Add friends and see each other’s live savings streaks — a little friendly motivation goes a long way toward building consistent habits.

⏰

Bill & payment reminders

Never miss a due date. Set one-off or repeating reminders that nudge you right on time, respecting your ringer profile and Do Not Disturb. Reminders stay private on your device.

🔁

Recurring income & payments

Add your salary, rent, subscriptions and other regular money, and let TINCAN apply them automatically on schedule so your books are always up to date.

🌍

Your currency, automatically

TINCAN shows amounts in your local currency based on your device’s region — no GPS, no location tracking — or pick any currency manually in Settings.

📴

Works fully offline

Don’t want an account? Use TINCAN as a guest. Everything stays on your device, works without internet, and seamlessly moves to your account if you decide to sign in later.

☁️

Sync across your devices

Sign in with Google or email to securely back up and sync your data across phones and tablets. Everything is encrypted in transit.

📊

PDF financial statements

Export a beautifully formatted financial statement — summary cards, charts and a full ledger — then save it or share it anywhere in a tap.

⚡

Widget & Quick Add

See your total and monthly balance right on your home screen, and add a transaction in just a couple of taps without even opening the app.
